About

Good Way To Start Good Day!

If you’re going to sit on your butt all day, at least leave your apartment first. theCoffeHouse brings a cozy, cabin-like quality to Brooklyn’s coffee scene, where visitors can borrow board games, flip through a selection of books, print the latest version of their resumes, and—most importantly for freelancers, study groups, and anyone else needing a work-conducive hideaway—sink into comfortable sofas for as long as they need.
